About Me

I am an incoming Assistant Professor at Rochester Institute of Technology Software Engineering department and currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow at Concordia University. I earned my Ph.D. at CUNY Graduate Center in 2021. My current research interests lie at software quality, empirical software engineering, and software logging.

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D., Master's and undergraduate students. Please contact me if you are interested in software engineering research.

I became interested in software engineering when I was a high school student. The first programming language I learned was Pascal. I still remember how it felt to learn programming and algorithms at that time, despite it being more than a decade ago. I am so happy that I am still insisting to keep pursuing my original dream. This will always be my goal and I hope to contribute more to the Software Engineering community.
